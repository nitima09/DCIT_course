Traffic jams are something that happens a lot in cities everywhere. They occur when there are too many cars on the road, and the traffic slows down or stops completely. This can be because of accidents, road construction, or just too many cars all trying to use the same road at once. Traffic jams can make people feel annoyed, make them late, and even cause them to miss important things like appointments. They can also be risky because drivers might get impatient and do dangerous things to try to get through the traffic.
